About

Oki, the charismatic CEO of Coded Bar, merges mixology and cookery, and by creating a captivating atmosphere with exquisite cocktails and immersive dishes, he leaves a lasting impression.

With its lounge, the bar hosts events, such as birthdays, karaokes and meetups, becoming a hub of entertainment. Oki's legacy is one of innovation and passion, forever influencing the world of mixology and cookery.
